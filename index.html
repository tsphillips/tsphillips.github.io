T. S. Phillips

I am a computer scientist and polymath, who, like many of my ilk, has numerous interests spanning both the creative and the technical. By creative, I mean implicated in the creation of novel works, some aesthetic and some not. By technical, I mean describing or derived from formal systems. My primary interests are language, computation, representation, and music.

I am the Chief Technical Officer at Ridgeback Network Defense, Inc., a cybersecurity company that focuses on deception technology and interactive defense.

Et Cetera

For my entire life I have created oodles of stuff, mostly computer software, games, and music. While I have knocked off numerous notebooks, prolifically programmed par polyglotte, and written reams of repertoire, I have produced a paucity of published works, staying mostly in the shadows.

Acknowledging the relentless churn of both technology and media, my focus has always been on the here and now, giving greater weight to what has utility at this time. All things being ephemeral, I rarely have had the desire to contribute to the mounting mass of media that, eventually, not even the libraries deem worthly of retention. Meanwhile, the most notable published authors, particularly those vested in academic careers, have taken great care to ensure their works are preserved. As I have grown older, I have begun to wonder, "hey, maybe I should catalog what I do."

And so, in this tiny corner of digital space, I include some links to things I have done, or am working on.
